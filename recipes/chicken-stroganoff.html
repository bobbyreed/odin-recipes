Chicken Stroganoff

Description

Ever wanted a single serving, warm cookie? Cookie in a Mug is your perfect solution.

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar - 2 Tablespoons
  • Coconut Oil - 1 Tablespoon (melted)
  • Salt - 1/4 Teaspoon
  • Flour - 4 Tablespoons
  • Dash of Vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar, coconut oil, and vanilla
  2. Add salt
  3. Stir in flour
  4. Microwave for 30-60 seconds (1100 watt microwave comes in at 60 seconds)

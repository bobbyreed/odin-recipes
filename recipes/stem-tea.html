Coconut Oil Stem Tea

Description

Why waste when you can reuse?

Ingredients

  • Dirty stem (I use the cooling unit from a Mighty)
  • Coconut oil
  • Double-boiler is nice, but not required

Steps

  1. Clean any non-resin debris from your stem
  2. Bring the coconut oil up to around 120 degrees
  3. Put the stem(s) in the coconut oil
  4. Maintain the temperature for 30 minutes
  5. Pull out the stems and clean them well. Leave no oil!
  6. Strain the liquid coconut oil through a coffee filter or cheese cloth

Annie's Aged Cheddar Shells and Cheese with Chicken

Description

This is a great boxed mac. With the right prep, it is a phenomenal and quick meal

Ingredients

  • Annie's Shells and White Cheddar - 1 box
  • Almond Milk - 3 Tablespoons
  • Butter - 2 Tablespoons
  • Dash of Black Pepper
  • Half of a can of Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil 5 cups of water in a medium sauce pan
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 - 11 minutes
  3. Drain pasta; Add butter and milk to hot empty pan
  4. Dump pasta on top of melting butter and milk. Stir until the butter is completely melted.
  5. Add 3/4 of the sauce packet
  6. Mix in the chicken
  7. Add the last 1/4 of the sauce packet
  8. Stir in Black Pepper
